Bookmarks are being saved in history though they shouldn't
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; de; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; de; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0

When clicking on the star in the adress bar accidentally and after that click "cancel", the bookmark is saved anyway in the "recently added bookmarks" folder.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open any website
2. Click the star in the adress bar and click cancel
3. Watch the folder "recently added bookmarks"
Actual Results:  
The non-existing bookmarks are shown in the folder "recently added bookmarks"

Expected Results:  
Nothing, the bookmark should not be shown anywhere.
