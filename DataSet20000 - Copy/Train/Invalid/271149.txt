Images do not properly conform to stylesheet under Firefox build 20041121
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8a5) Gecko/20041121 Firefox/0.9.1+
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8a5) Gecko/20041121 Firefox/0.9.1+

If you observe http://www.spreadfirefox.com at 8:30PM EST with Firefox 0.9.1+
build 20041121, the images will be overlapping the third column (on the right)
with a screen resolution of 1024x768 or under. 

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to www.spreadfirefox.com

Actual Results:  
Images in second column will overlap the third column at a resolution of
1024x768 or under.

Expected Results:  
Images should either have shrank to the appropriate size or should have
continued the page with a horizontal scrollbar.
