Adding a second badge doubles up the previously added one

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to Firefox 4 Facebook page
2. Click on 1 of 4 badges to add to your wall
3. Add comment and click Publish
4. Repeat step 2
5. Add another comment to the post and Publish it
6. Refresh your news feed page
Actual Results:  
Badge doubles up the previously added one

Expected Results:  
News feed page on Facebook shows up individual posts
