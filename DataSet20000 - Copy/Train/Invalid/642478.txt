When hiding a post we expect only it to be hidden (following Facebook rules), but actually all firefox4 posts become hidded.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
Prerequisites: 2 Facebook users that are friends on Facebook (i.e. UserA and UserB)

Steps:
1. Go to http://www.facebook.com/pages/Firefox-4/130379370362344?sk=app_122300121174636
2. UserA - Post more than 1 badge
3. UserB - in News Feeds -> Most recent  -> select one of the posts and click on its "Remove" button (the X button) 
4. UserB - Select "Hide this post" ==> Expected\Actual result#1
5. UserB - After the post is hidden - Click on News Feed
6. UserB - Go to Top News and Most Recent pages ==> Expected\Actual result#2
Actual Results:  
** Actual result#1
Only the selected post was hidden in UserB News feed pages

** Actual result#2:
All Firefox badges posts were hidden from UserB Top News and Most Recent pages



Expected Results:  
** Expected result#1:
Only the selected post will be hidden in UserB News Feed page

** Expected result#1:
Only the selected post will be hidden in UserB Top News and News Feed pages
