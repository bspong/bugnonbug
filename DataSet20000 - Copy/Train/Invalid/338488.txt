Zoom box does not keep up with cursor
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.0.3) Gecko/20060426 Firefox/1.5.0.3
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.0.3) Gecko/20060426 Firefox/1.5.0.3

If you use the new zoom feature and drag your mouse around quickly, the zoom window does not "follow along"

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Double-click on the image to open the zoom window
2. Move the mouse around quickly
Actual Results:  
The mouse cursor changes to a zoom-plus-sign and the "zoom box" does not "follow along"

Expected Results:  
The "zoom box" might get sick to its stomach, but it still follows along
