When I edit an address in the address bar and press enter, the changes are lost and the old address is loaded.  

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Type in an address and go to that site.
2. Edit the address
3. press enter

Actual Results:  
Say I had http://www.website.com/subsite/ and I change it to http://www.website.com/ and press enter, I goto http://www.website.com/subsite, not http://www.website.com/

Expected Results:  
Goto http://www.website.com/ when I edit the address and press enter.
