No Right-click on Google Toolbar Bookmarks entries in Mac OS X
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; en-US; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; en-US; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6

Previous versions of Firefox allowed display of right-click options when accessing entries in Google Bookmarks (such as open in new window or new tab). This appears to have been disabled. 

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click on Google Bookmarks
2. Navigate to individual Google Bookmarks entry
3. Right-click using mouse or pad.
Actual Results:  
Nothing happens.

Expected Results:  
Menu with several options (open in new window, open in new tab, etc.)
