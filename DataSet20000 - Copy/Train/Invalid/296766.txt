Ctrl+Enter on location bar ignores keywords
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050421 Firefox/1.0.4 (MOOX M2)
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050421 Firefox/1.0.4 (MOOX M2)

When typing a keyword search in the Location bar, Ctrl+Enter doesn't open in a
new tab, and ignores the keyword, appending http://www. and .com/ to the name.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Type "google mozilla" in the location bar
2.Press Ctrl+Enter

Actual Results:  
The URL bar changes to http://www.google mozilla.com/

Expected Results:  
A new tab opens with a Google search for "mozilla".
