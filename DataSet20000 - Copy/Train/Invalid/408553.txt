Gmail Logo is not visible after session restore
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1.11) Gecko/20071127 Firefox/2.0.0.11
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1.11) Gecko/20071127 Firefox/2.0.0.11

If you were restoring a session of gmail, the logo and some images do not always load. However after quiting and again running firefox fixes the problem.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Run Gmail
2. Crash or close forecefully firefox
3. Use session restore to view gmail
Actual Results:  
the logo does not appear

Expected Results:  
the gmail logo should appear.
