If browser is opened before the midnight and you surf after the midnight,
history bar is not updated. All links appear if you close it and reopen again.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the browser before midnight
2. Visit couple of pages after midnight
Actual Results:  
The history bar is not updated

Expected Results:  
Today pages should switch to yesterday, which would make place for real today
visited pages to appear on the screen.
