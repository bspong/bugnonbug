Problem with users
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-GB; rv:1.9.0.4) Gecko/2008102920 Firefox/3.0.4
Build Identifier: Firefox 2.1

Firefox forget all created users.

Reproducible: Didn't try

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Firefox.
2. After few hours maybe you will have luck and you will have to close it.
3. You wont be able to open it again.

Actual Results:  
If you want to open it again, then you will have to kill Firefox process in Task Manager. After that you will loose all users.
