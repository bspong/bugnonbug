Random screen switch when opening new tab with scrollwheel
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0.1) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0.1) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0.1

Sometimes when opening a new tab scroll-clicking an empty space of the tab bar, firefox would suddenly move to the second monitor and save it's position there on every start until firefox is restored, moved back to monitor #1 and closed.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Get 2 screens
2. Open a new tab by scroll-clicking the tab bar

Actual Results:  
Sometimes, a new tab will open and firefox jumps to screen #2 and stays there until firefox is restored, moved back and closed.

Expected Results:  
A new tab should be opened without firefox changing screens
