Cannot open a new tab or window when the link is an image
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Win98; en-US; rv:1.4a) Gecko/20030323 Phoenix/0.5
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Win98; en-US; rv:1.4a) Gecko/20030323 Phoenix/0.5

Basically you cannot create a new tab or window when you right-click on an image
(that is a hyperlink) and choose either: new window or new tab

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Find any image with a hyperlink
2. Right-click over the image
3. Attempt to click on new tab or new window

Actual Results:  
Nothing happens (that is the problem)

Expected Results:  
Opened a new tab or window
