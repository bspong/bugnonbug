when the user wants to change the privacy option of a specific message they are able to use the little "lock" button to do that. they should be able to return to the "post on wall" page once the save the settings and continue the posting funnel.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1) Go to badges page and click on a badge -> the "Post to Wall" window appears.
2) click on the little "lock" icon on the bottom left corner -> chose "Custom" on the little window that appears.
3) on the "Custom Privacy" window choose any option you like and click "save settings".
Actual Results:  
The "Custom Privacy" window disappears and that is it. No post to wall. See attached video capture.

Expected Results:  
The "Custom Privacy" window will disappear and the "Post to Wall" window returns.
