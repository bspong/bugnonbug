The search bar uses Alt+Enter to open searches in a new tab instea of Cmd+Enter. On platform Mac, loads in new tabs all happen on Cmd+Enter, except in the search bar, which seems to use Alt+Enter.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Type text in search bar
2. Press Cmd+Enter
Actual Results:  
Results load in current tab.

Expected Results:  
Results load in new tab.
