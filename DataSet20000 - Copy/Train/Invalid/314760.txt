Text Area disappears when Shift+Double Click
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b5) Gecko/20051006 Firefox/1.4.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b5) Gecko/20051006 Firefox/1.4.1

When pressing and holding Shift, and double-clicking on a word in a text area, the text area disappears!
I have experienced this with Yahoo! Mail, when replying to a message. I haven't tried it elsewhere.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Yahoo Mail, and reply to a message.
2. In the text area, press and hold Shift and Double Click on a word.

Actual Results:  
Text area disappears!

Expected Results:  
text selection
