User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.0; en-US; rv:1.4a) Gecko/20030423 Firebird Browser/0.6
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.0; en-US; rv:1.4a) Gecko/20030423 Firebird Browser/0.6

When composing mail with Yahoo mail and using the address book to insert
addresses into the To, Cc, or Bcc fields, the addresses do not get inserted. 
This used to work in older versions of Phoenix/Mozilla.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Yahoo mail (mail.yahoo.com)
2. Click on "Compose"
3. Click on "Insert addresses"
4. Select a address by clicking in the "To" box
5. Click on "Insert Checked Contacts"

Actual Results:  
Nothing gets inserted into the "To" field.

Expected Results:  
Insert the address in the "To" field.
