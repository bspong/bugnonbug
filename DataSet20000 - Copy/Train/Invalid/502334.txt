User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9.1) Gecko/20090624 Firefox/3.5

In Tools/Options/Privacy when Location Bar Suggest setting is changed for something other than "History and Bookmarks", and after FF restart this setting is still set to "History and Bookmarks".

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Go to Tools/Options/Privacy and change Location Bar Suggest to something other than "History and Bookmarks"
2.Restart Firefox
3.Go to Tools/Options/Privacy and Location Bar Suggest it's still set to "History and Bookmarks"
