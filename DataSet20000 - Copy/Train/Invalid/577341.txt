No minimize/maximize/close button in Firefox 4.0 beta 1
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; en-US; rv:2.0b1) Gecko/20100630 Firefox/4.0b1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; en-US; rv:2.0b1) Gecko/20100630 Firefox/4.0b1

When running Firefox 4.0 beta 1 it can be noticed that there appears no minimize/maximize or close button in the right upper corner of the window. This makes resizing the window impossible in Windows 7.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Launch Firefox 4.0 beta 1 (run in 1280 x 800 resolution)
2. Try to find the minimize/maximize/close button in the right upper corner of the screen/window

Actual Results:  
No minimize/maximize/close button available in right upper corner of the window/screen.

Expected Results:  
Buttons for minimizing/maximizing/closing the window.
