Addressbar is not updated after clicking on a bookmark link

The url showed in a addressbar is not updated after a click on a bookmark link

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Surf to a webpage (eg www.google.com) --> Webpage is shown and the addressbar shows google.com
2. Click on a bookmark link pointing to reader.google.com (url doesn't matter)
--> 
a) reader Webpage is shown
b) Certificate next to the url shows the new Certficate (e.g. Google reader)
c) url in the addressbar still shows the old address (e.g. www.google.com)
d) title in the tab is correct and shows the new webpage title
Actual Results:  
Addressbar is not updated and shows the previous address

Expected Results:  
Addressbar is updated and shows the current url
