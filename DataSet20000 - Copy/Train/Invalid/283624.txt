New Options will not display with themes other than 'default'
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b2) Gecko/20050225 Firefox/1.0+
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b2) Gecko/20050225 Firefox/1.0+

Appears that the landing of the New Options GUI will not work with any existing
themes other than 'Default'.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Use theme other than default
2. Open Tools/Options 
3. Note NO display of Options Box, and only OK and Cancel show in lower right
corner of screen, clicking 'cancel' closes the buttons.

Actual Results:  
Only OK and Cancel button displayed in lower right corner of screen.

Expected Results:  
The Options GUI should have opened like any other option GUI, ie:
themes/extensions/downloads etc...
