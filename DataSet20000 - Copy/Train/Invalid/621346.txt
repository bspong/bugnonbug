Known addons such as Firebug (1.7X.0a7) have buttons on the customize toolbar window that worked on FF4b7. Those icons now are not showing.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Install Firebug (1.7X.0a7)
2. Menu View > Toolbars > Customize
Actual Results:  
The custom icons (from any addon) will not show up.

Expected Results:  
To see two icons: firebug.Firebug and firebug.Inspect

All settings to default.
