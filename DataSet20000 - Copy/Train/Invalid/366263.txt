- On all wikipedia pages no images are displayed

- no image in the image page (eg. http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Image:Flag_of_Japan.svg)

- if the address of the image is entered directly, the image IS displayed (eg. http://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/9e/Flag_of_Japan.svg)

- this as the case for all image formats I found (.jpg, .png, .svg)

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. open any wikipedia page with an image on it
Actual Results:  
no images

Expected Results:  
images

- does happen in safe mode
- does happen with firefox-3.0a2pre.en-US.mac
- does NOT happen with Seamonkey (images load fine there)
- does NOT happen for any other pages in firefox as far as I'm aware of.
