Ive answered a support question with detailed reproduction of this fault. https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/questions/825628#answer-202340

Depending how you close firefox depends on whether the App Tabs restore.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Close firefox window with top left X (on macs) Firefox remains open in menu bar below
2. Right click firefox icon - select QUIT
3. Apps gone (if you have firefox open and not step 1 - apps dont go). If you use Firefox Menu (top left of desktop) then QUIT - app tabs stay.

Actual Results:  
App tabs vanish.

Expected Results:  
App tabs dont vanish.
