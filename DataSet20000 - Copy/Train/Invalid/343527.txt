Shortcut key doesn't on Download History drop-down in preferences
There is one shortcut key doesn't work in firefox preferences tab.

Steps to reproduce:
1. Launch firefox.
2. Select top menu Edit->Preferences->Privacy Tab->Download History Tab.
3. Press Alt+E to move focus onto combobox.

Expected result:
Foucs should be on the combobox widget after step 3.

Actual result:
Nothing happens after step 3. The shortcut key Alt+E will not work at all.

This bug can be found with firefox 2.0a3 on solaris nevada_43/vermillion_44 and ubunto.
