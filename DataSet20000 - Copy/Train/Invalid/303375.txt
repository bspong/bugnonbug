When middlemouse.contentLoadURL=true, on my SuSe 9.3, firefox open's new browser
window minimized in taskbar (regular left click on link, thats should open in
new window). When "false", all is ok.

Maybe it's KDE problem. KDE 3.4.2.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Set middlemouse.contentLoadURL=true.
2. Click on any link (new window link of course) or select "Open Link in new
window" (right click on link).

Actual Results:  
Firefox window open's minimized in taskbar.

Expected Results:  
Normaly (not minimized) open new window.
