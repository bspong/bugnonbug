Bookmarks and history disappear [caused by the Google Toolbar add-on]
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0.1) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0.1) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0.1

History is not always saved (see reproducibility).
New bookmarks are sometimes lost after closing Firefox.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open any new page in the new tab (not in the history).
2. Close tab.
3. Try to enter part of the address or page name in the address field.


Actual Results:  
No suggestion or history is present.

Expected Results:  
FF should remember page that was visited few seconds ago.
