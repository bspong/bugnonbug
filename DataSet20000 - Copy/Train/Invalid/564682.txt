localStorage is not persistent on Linux (Ubuntu). Stored values are lost when closing FF, and not present next time FF is opened. This happens for any cookie persistence configuration. I have been unable to find any other relevant configuration option.

Tested on Ubuntu 10.04-x86_64 and 9.10-i386. On Windows, localStorage works just fine.
