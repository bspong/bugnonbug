Tab strip would not scroll when I dragged a tab of other browsers.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:

1. Open more than 2 browsers
2. Open several tabs in order to overflow tab strip.
3. Drag a tab to the overflowed tab strip.
   And try to scroll the strip.

Actual Results: 
  The overflowed tab strip does not scroll.

Expected Results: 
  The overflowed tab strip should scroll.
