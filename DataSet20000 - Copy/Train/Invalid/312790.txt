New Tab Button doesn't show on Tab Bar whether the option is checked or not
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b5) Gecko/20051006 Firefox/1.4.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b5) Gecko/20051006 Firefox/1.4.1

Whether the option 'Show the New Tab button on the Tab Bar' from the Display
Options category of Tabbed Browsing in the Options dialog box is selected or
not, the New Tab button fails to show up on the Tab Bar. In case this is a
problem caused by an extension, let me list the extensions related to tabbed
browsing I am using: LastTab 1.1.1; Tabbrowser Preferences 1.2.8.5; and FLST 0.8.5

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Go to Options from the Tools menu.
2.Select Tabbed Browsing and then Display Options
3.Check (if not already) 'Show the New Tab button on the Tab Bar'

Actual Results:  
The New Tab button showed have been displayed on the Tab Bar.

Expected Results:  
The New Tab button failed to show up.
