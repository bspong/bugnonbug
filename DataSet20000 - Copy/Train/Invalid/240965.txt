Tabbing through entry fields on page causes crash
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.6) Gecko/20040116
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.6) Gecko/20040210 Firefox/0.8

Tabbing through the registration page of VRVS [www.vrvs.org -> START ->
REGISTER] after filling in the fields from the top causes Firefox to hang after
tabbing from the 'Phone Number' option.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Browse to www.vrvs.org -> START -> REGISTER
2. Fill in fields from top using 'tab' key to go on
3. Crash as moving to field after 'Phone number'

Actual Results:  
Firefox locks up.  No screen redraws.

Expected Results:  
Gone onto the next selection.
