Tab bar in Firefox 2 for Mac makes entire Firefox window unusable
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0

When the tab bar is visible in Firefox 2 for Mac, the "usable" portion of that Firefox window shrinks down to a 1-inch wide section on the left hand side.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Make tab bar visible

Actual Results:  
"Usable" portion of browser window shrinks to a 1-inch wide section on the left.

Expected Results:  
Entire browser window should be usable.
