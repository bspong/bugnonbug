go on bookmarks library, perform a search that involves the history (e.g.: last 7 days) and try to save the search in your bookmarks.

The saved search won't be able to show anything.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. from bookmarks library perform a search for "goo" in your bookmarks (this is assuming you have, for example, google in your bookmarks)
2.change "containing the text" to "visited" during the last 7 days.
3.click "save" and give any name to the "smart bookmark"
Actual Results:  
the smart bookmark is always empty, no matter what you search for.

Expected Results:  
smart bookmark showing "google", assuming you visited that website during the last 7 days

another related bug shows up following the next steps:

1. from bookmarks library perform a search for "goo" in your bookmarks (this is assuming you have, for example, google in your bookmarks)
2.change "containing the text" to "visited" during the last 7 days.
3.click "+" to add another criteria, but leave it empty (the result pane will be empty)
