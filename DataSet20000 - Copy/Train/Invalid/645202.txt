Tab title text disappears [Caused by Anti Aliasing Tuner]
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0

Sometimes, the names on tabs will disappear. This is new with Firefox 4.0.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open a new tab. Middle clicking a shortcut bar item is the easiest way.
2. Observe if any titles are missing.
3. Continue opening new tabs until some are seen with missing titles.
Actual Results:  
Only some of the tabs have titles.

Expected Results:  
All tabs have titles.

1600x900 screen resolution, win7 Aero, maximized window. Tends to happen when I open the 10th tab.
