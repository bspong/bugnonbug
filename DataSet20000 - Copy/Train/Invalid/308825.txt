Location bar does not accept mouse click
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b4) Gecko/20050915 Firefox/1.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b4) Gecko/20050915 Firefox/1.4

Clicking in the location bar does not change the text cursor position in it.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Load a page from the bookmarks
2. Click in the location bar

Actual Results:  
The text cursor is located at the beginning of the location bar.

Expected Results:  
The text cursor should be at the clicked position.

This problem also appears in Preferences->General->Home Page text field and
other UI text fields.
