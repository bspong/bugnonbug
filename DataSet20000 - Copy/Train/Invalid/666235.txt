When the Tab Bar is on top of the address bar, it gets its color settings from a different source than when below.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.  Right click on tab bar.
2.  Click on 'Tabs on Top'.

Actual Results:  
Tabs move to other side of address bar and change color.

Expected Results:  
Tabs move to other side of address bar.

http://i.imgur.com/9ldhb.png

For my particular theme, my tab bar is light grey when it's on the bottom and dark grey when it's above.
