Showing Facebook sidebar completely freezes Firefox (permanently, afaict)
STR:

* Enable Facebook messenger for Firefox
* Click the "F" icon
* Click "show sidebar"

Result:

* We open the sidebar window and load most of its content.  (There's a bit missing towards the bottom.)  Then Firefox freezes completely.
