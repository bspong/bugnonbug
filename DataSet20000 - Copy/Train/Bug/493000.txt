Title bar does not be hidden in Full Screen Mode after restart Minefield.


Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Start SMinefield with new profile.
2. You must confirm that there are two or three tabs.
3. Maximize main-window.
4. Turn into FullScreen by press F11 key.
5. Quit Shiretoko by the upper right X button in window-contorols.(Chose "Save and Quit", when the warning dialogue was displayed)
6. Restart Minefield.
7. Turn into FullScreen by press F11 key.

Actual Results:  
Title bar does not be hidden.

Expected Results:  
Title bar should be hidden in Full Screen Mode.

After Strep 7. of the above.
8.Turn into Normal Screen by press F11 key.

Actual Result
The Status bar is covered by the Task Bar of the windows.

Expected Results
The Status bar must not be covered.
