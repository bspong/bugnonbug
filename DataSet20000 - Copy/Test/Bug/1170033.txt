Ghost tabs appear when opening new tab immediately after tab or window close
Created attachment 8613318
Sample image (mouse cursor was atop ghost tab to show outline)

This is hard to reproduce precisely, however I can do it 1 - 2 times per week. It seems to happen when I open a new tab via Ctrl+T rapidly after closing another tab or window. I think (but am not 100% sure) that the Ctrl+T must occur while the closing tab/window is still animating.
