When editing a text input field that can take multiple  , if I write a few lines in an RTL language inside an LTR field (or vice versa) and go back to a previous line and add to it, the letters of the last line will sometimes become reversed.

It happened to me in both English and Arabic, in Google Reader comments and in a vBulletin forum.

It's hard to reproduce, but it happened to me and other people like Khaled Hosny:

https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/show_bug.cgi?id=499538#c60

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Type multiple lines of an RTL language in a LTR field (or vice versa)
2. Go back and add a few words in a previous line
3. The last line will sometimes become reversed.
