Full Screen Navigation Icons Do Not Remember Large/Small Setting

Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.6)

If you make your navigation icons large, upon fullscreening via F11, the icons 
revert to small. You can then make them large, but this setting is not 
remembered. The icon ordering apparently is remembered.
