Accelerate key needed in Bookmarks menu.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050607 Firefox/1.0.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060213 Firefox/1.6a1

This is a accessibility bug of firefox.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Run firefox.
2. Press Alt+B to open bookmarks menu.


Actual Results:  
The menu items listed below Bookmarks have no accelerate keys.

Expected Results:  
The menu items listed below Bookmarks should have their accelerate keys.

This bug can be seen in "Go" menu.
