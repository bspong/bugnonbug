Recently Closed Tabs doesn't show site closed with command-W
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.5; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008061004 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.5; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008061004 Firefox/3.0


Under the menu History, Recently Closed Tabs, the page are not listed if closed with Command-W, they are only listed if they are closed with the mouse.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open any website.
2. Press Command-W
3. Try to open the site again through Recently Closed Tabs, and it's not there, you have to find it in History.
