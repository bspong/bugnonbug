If there are too many bookmark on the bookmark bar, the hidden one can't be removed.
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0

If there are too many bookmark on the bookmark bar, one'll need to click on a button to show the hidden one that couldn't fit on the visible bar. On this menu, one can right-click and perform operation on these bookmarks. However, trying to remove the bookmark will not delete it.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Add several page to the bookmark bar, until they're hidden.
2. Click on the button that show the hidden bookmarks.
3. Right-click it and choose to remove one of them.
Actual Results:
Nothing happens.

Expected Results:
The bookmark should be removed from the bookmark bar.
