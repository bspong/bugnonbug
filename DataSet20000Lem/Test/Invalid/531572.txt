Middle-click on tab bar is not working a expected [caused by TabMixPlus]
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; fr; rv:1.9.1.4) Gecko/20091016 Firefox/3.5.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; fr; rv:1.9.1.4) Gecko/20091016 Firefox/3.5.4

If you middle-click on tab in the tab toolbar, it close the tab.
But, if you middle-click on a free space in the tab toolbar, it reopens previously closed tab instead opening a new one.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Close a tab (middle-click or close button)
2. Middle-click on the toolbar to restore this tab
Actual Results:
Middle-click on the toolbar restores a previously closed tab.

Expected Results:
Middle-click on the toolbar should open a new tab.
