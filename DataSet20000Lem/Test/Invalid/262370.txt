if you were to have a certain link, such a <a
href="http://bugzilla.mozilla.org/">Bugzilla</a>, and tried to drag it onto the
Bookmarks Toolbar, nothing would happen. But other links, for example, <a
href="https://bugzilla.mozilla.org/">Bugzilla</a> or <a
href="http://www.bugzilla.org">Bugzilla</a> work fine. Another link that doe
not work is <a href="http://webmail.earthlink.net">Earthlink Mail</a>. I have no
idea what cause this. It can be easily worked around by right-clicking the link
and choosing Bookmark This Link... and putting it in the Bookmarks Toolbar Folder.

Reproducible: Always

1. Go to Google and search for "Mozilla.org: Bugzilla"
2. Try to drag the link that point to http://bugzilla.mozilla.org onto the
Bookmarks Toolbar

Actual Results:
Nothing happens

Expected Results:
Created a button on the toolbar for the link
