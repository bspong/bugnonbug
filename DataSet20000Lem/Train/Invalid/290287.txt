Closing one other tab brings up multiple tab confirmation dialog
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.6) Gecko/20050317 Firefox/1.0.2
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.6) Gecko/20050317 Firefox/1.0.2

If you have only 2 tab open and you right-click one and choose "close other
tabs", it come up with the close multiple tab dialog, even though you're only
closing one.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open one additional tab (for 2 total)
2. Right-click one and choose "close other tabs".
Actual Results:
It come up with the close multiple tab dialog.

Expected Results:
Closed the tab without a dialog.
