Adware.Navipromo.AO rootkit cause Firefox to hang and stop loading page
Extremely common on Mozilla Italian forums, there's a virus going around that is installing with various software package and causing Firefox to hang or stop loading pages. Closing Firefox doe not kill the process and user get a "Firefox is already running but not responding" message when they try to start again. This only affect Firefox, Chrome is unaffected a is Explorer.

After extensive and detailed detective work: http://www.bitdefender.com/VIRUS-1000183-en--Adware.Navipromo.AO.html ha been identified a the cause.
