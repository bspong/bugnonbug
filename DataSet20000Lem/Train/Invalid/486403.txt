Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open firefox and ensure Google toolbar is installed.
2. Enter text into search box on left hand side of toolbar and press enter
3.
Actual Results:
Search Box will remain dropped down and open, and will remain on screen even if Firefox is minimised.
