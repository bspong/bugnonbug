Bookmarks toolbar folders: bookmark cannot be opened by clicking, no right click either
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008051206 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008051206 Firefox/3.0

FF 3.0 RC1. Bookmarks placed into a folder in the Toolbar cannot be opened by clicking on them. Right-clicking a bookmark close the drop-down list of bookmark in the folder. Only way to open these bookmark is by clicking on the folder and using the arrow key to scroll to the bookmark, then press enter key.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Create a folder for bookmark within the "Bookmarks Toolbar"
2. Right click or left click on a bookmark
3. Drop-down menu close without redirecting to bookmark.
Actual Results:
Nothing.

Expected Results:
Should redirect to the bookmark. Other bookmark placed a stand-alone entry in Bookmarks Toolbar work a expected.
