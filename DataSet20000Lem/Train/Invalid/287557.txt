Controls functionless on BBC Radio Player under Firefox 1.0.2 but OK on IE 6.0
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-GB; rv:1.7.6) Gecko/20050321 Firefox/1.0.2
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-GB; rv:1.7.6) Gecko/20050321 Firefox/1.0.2

The control button ("Play", "Pause", "Volume Up" and "Volume Down" controls)
don't work on the BBC Radio Player. The button work fine in IE 6.0. Steps to
reproduce the problem detailed below

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio1/listen/index.shtml?hp_lhn. and press
"Listen Live". Alternatively, go to http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio/aod/radio1.shtml
2. Let the page load and the streaming radio begin. Click on "P(lay" top left of
window). This should change to a "Pause" button and pause the broadcast.
3. Additionally, the Volume Controls (again, top left) should change the volume
and the meter should reflect the volume.

Actual Results:
Controls work fine in IE 6.0 but do nothing in Firefox 1.0.1 or 1.0.2.

Expected Results:
As per "Steps to Reproduce".
