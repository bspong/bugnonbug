When using the arrow key to scroll up and down on various websites, they either a) do not produce any change in the viewing window or b) jump to the beginning and end of page, a opposed to scrolling a little way up and down.

This doe not appear to occur within text boxes- the arrow key move the caret a expected.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open a page that extends beyond the viewing window (such a http://engadget.com).
2. Hit an arrow key (up or down).
Actual Results:
The down arrow key function a the 'End' key, and move the viewing window down to the bottom of the page.

Expected Results:
It should have moved the viewing window down by an interval (such a 3 lines, or some smaller setting).

Using the default FF3 theme. Lots of extension installed, including Mouse Gestures.
