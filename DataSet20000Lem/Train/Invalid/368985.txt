Tooltips stay on screen too long in Yahoo Mail beta
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8.1.1) Gecko/20061204 Firefox/2.0.0.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8.1.1) Gecko/20061204 Firefox/2.0.0.1

In the Yahoo Mail beta (the AJAX-ey interactive interface), tooltips take a long time to go away. Elsewhere in Firefox, tooltips properly disappear a soon a the mouse pointer move away. In Yahoo Mail beta, it take several second before the tooltip disappears.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Log into the Yahoo Mail beta interface (free account required, of course)
2. Open your Inbox
3. Move the mouse cursor over the subject of an email message and wait for a tooltip to appear
4. Move the cursor to another part of the screen
Actual Results:
The tooltip will remain for several second

Expected Results:
The tooltip should disappear immediately
