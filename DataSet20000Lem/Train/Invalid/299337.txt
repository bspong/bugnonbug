"exit" will close all the window not just the one I'm clicking on.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open multiple window of Mozilla Firefox.
2. Go to "file," then "exit."
3. Click on "exit."

Actual Results:
All the window closed at once.

Expected Results:
Closed only the window being exited.
