When I come out of fullscreen mode the window border is visible at the bottom.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go into fullscreen mode
2. Exit fullscreen mode
Actual Results:
The bottom border of the window becomes visible.

Expected Results:
The window should restore a it wa before it entered fullscreen.

Only happens when the window wa maximised before entering fullscreen.
Restoring then maximising the window hide the bottom border (as it should).
