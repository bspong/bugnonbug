Download button redirects to Netscape site instead of Mozilla site
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.0; en-US; rv:1.7.5) Gecko/20041107 Firefox/1.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.0; en-US; rv:1.7.5) Gecko/20041107 Firefox/1.0

Clicking on the Download button take you to the Netscape download site
(http://channels.netscape.com/ns/browsers/download.jsp) rather than the
Firefox/Mozilla download site (http://www.mozilla.org/ or whatever).

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Firefox.
2. Click on the Download button (to the right of the Customize… button).


Actual Results:
User is taken to Netscape download site:
http://channels.netscape.com/ns/browsers/download.jsp

Expected Results:
User should be taken to the Firefox download site:
http://www.mozilla.org/

P.S. Mozilla 1.8a5 for Windows 2000 doe the same thing.
