"Open in Tab" close extra tabs.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open up 4 page in 4 different tab (and visit any site that you want).
2. You now have 4 tab open.
3. In your bookmark find a folder with two URLs. Click "Open in New Tabs"
Actual Results:
The last 2 tab are closed, and the link open in the first two tabs.

Expected Results:
The other tab should not have been closed.
