Holding down SHIFT while double-clicking text to highlight it cause that text to disappear.

Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8b4) Gecko/20050908 Firefox/1.4

The text between line break (<BR> OR <P>) disappears when you hold down SHIFT
and double-click text (as if selecting a whole line). This also cause image to
disappear. The text is unrecoverable, unless you refresh the page.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to website with text
2. Hold down shift and double-click on any word


Actual Results:
A block of text (maybe images) will disappear.

Expected Results:
Highlighted the current line.
