Opening and activating AOM tab decrease tab-bar's height
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; rv:2.0b13pre) Gecko/20110223 Firefox/4.0b13pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; rv:2.0b13pre) Gecko/20110223 Firefox/4.0b13pre

Opening and activating AOM tab decrease tab-bar's height, so if you cyclically switch to AOM from regular tab and back - you'll notice tab-bar bouncing.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open 2 tab
2. In 1 of them open about:addons page.
3. CTRL-TAB many times.
Actual Results:
Tab-bar's height get changed after every CTRL+TAB hit.

Expected Results:
Tab-bar's height should remain the same.
