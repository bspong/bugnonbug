Drag a tab from an open page out of the tab area and let go, it make a new page and the tab name come along for the ride. That is good. However, when you drag the tab from the new page back into the original page, it set into the page with the tab name, “(Untitled)”.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Start Firefox 3.5.5, make a new tab and go anywhere.
2.Drag a new tab out to make a new page.
3.Drag the tab from new page back to original page.
Actual Results:
Tab Naming Error. The name of the tab is, (Untitled).

Expected Results:
The name of the tab should follow the tab to any page it is draged to.
