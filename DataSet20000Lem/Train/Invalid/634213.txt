If master password is enabled with hidden menu bar, tab are not merged to the title bar [caused by Tab Mix Plus]
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11

If the master password option is set in the preference and the menu bar is hidden, after restarting Firefox, the tab are no longer merged in the title bar.

The "Firefox" button is still in the title bar, though.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Hide the menu bar
2. Enable master password
3. Restart Firefox

Actual Results:
Tabs are displayed under the title bar

Expected Results:
Tabs merged into the title bar
