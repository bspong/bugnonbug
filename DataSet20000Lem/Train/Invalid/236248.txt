Toolbar and menu bar item do not work when Search field ha focus
User-Agent:
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.6) Gecko/20040207 Firefox/0.8

In the Bookmarks Manager folder, if the Search field ha the focus, the toolbar
and menu bar item are enabled but do not respond when clicked.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Start Firefox
2. Select Bookmarks->Manage Bookmarks... menu.
3. Click into the Search text field (below the toolbar).
4. Click any enabled toolbar or menu bar item (e.g. New Folder...).

Actual Results:
Nothing.

Expected Results:
Whatever I clicked on (for the above example: a new bookmark folder get created).
