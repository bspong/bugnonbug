When you click on the white space on some page the div margin start blinking a a cursor. This wasn't visible on earlier versions, now I found this on many pages.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
Create a simple div with a width/height given.
<!DOCTYPE html PUBLIC "-//W3C//DTD XHTML 1.0 Transitional//EN" "http://www.w3.org/TR/xhtml1/DTD/xhtml1-transitional.dtd">
<html>
</body>
<div style="width:300px;height:200px;background-color:yellow"></div>
</body>
</html>
Actual Results:
Clicking at the whitespace anywhere outside the div, the margin of the div start blinking.

Expected Results:
The blinking should disappear.
