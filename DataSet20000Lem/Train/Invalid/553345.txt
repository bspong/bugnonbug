No Right-click on Google Toolbar Bookmarks entry in Mac OS X
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; en-US; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; en-US; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6

Previous version of Firefox allowed display of right-click option when accessing entry in Google Bookmarks (such a open in new window or new tab). This appears to have been disabled.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click on Google Bookmarks
2. Navigate to individual Google Bookmarks entry
3. Right-click using mouse or pad.
Actual Results:
Nothing happens.

Expected Results:
Menu with several option (open in new window, open in new tab, etc.)
