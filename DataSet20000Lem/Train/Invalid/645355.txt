Private Browsing deletes history even after returning to normal mode
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0

After closing private browsing (through the tool menu), it still doesn't save history.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to private browsing.
2. Close private browsing from the tool menu.
3. Go to any website.
4. Open history manager.
Actual Results:
The website we visited in step 3 is not in the history.

Expected Results:
The website we visited in step 3 should be recorded in the history.
