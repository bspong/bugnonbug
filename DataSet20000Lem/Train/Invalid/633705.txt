Middle clicking on an item on the bookmark bar or the nav bar open in foreground tab
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11

Middle clicking on an item from the bookmark bar or the navigation bar (e.g. homepage button, back button, any link in the dropped-down url bar etc.) will result in opening said link in a new foreground tab, instead of the firefox 3.6 behaviour of opening it in a new background tab.
Middle clicking on a link from a page produce the normal result.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open firefox
2. Middle click on any item in the bookmark bar or the navigation bar
3. The item open in a foreground tab
Actual Results:
The item open in a foreground tab

Expected Results:
The item should have opened in a background tab
