Bookmarks imported from Opera 9.x are inserted into Bookmarks Toolbar
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.2; en-US; rv:1.8.0.7) Gecko/20060831 Firefox/1.5.0.7
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.2; en-US; rv:1.8.0.7) Gecko/20060831 Firefox/1.5.0.7

After importing bookmark from Opera 9, most of them are inserted into the Bookmarks Toolbar automatically and not inside a seperate folder for Opera in the bookmark list.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. File | Import...
2. Select Opera and click Next.
3. Unselect all option except Bookmarks and click Next.
4. Click Finish.

Actual Results:
Bookmarks are added into the Bookmarks Toolbar and two folders, inside the 'From Opera' folder: Keyword Searches (From Opera) and Trash are present in the Bookmarks list.

Expected Results:
Bookmarks imported should be in a Opera folder inside the Bookmarks list, not the toolbar.
