User-Agent:
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7a) Gecko/20040212 Firebird/0.8.0+

additional key (Enter) press.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Press Alt+f to open File menu
2. Press mnemonic a.

Actual Results:
Nothing happens.

Expected Results:
Page saving dialog should appear.
