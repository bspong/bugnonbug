Items dragged and dropped on to the Add-on bar are not restored
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b8pre) Gecko/20101130 Firefox/4.0b8pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b8pre) Gecko/20101130 Firefox/4.0b8pre

Items dragged and dropped on to the Add-on bar are not restored to their original position after resetting

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Bring up the Add-on Bar (View->Toolbar->Add-on Bar)
2.Bring up the Customize Toolbar Dialog (View->Toolbar->Customize...)
3.Now drag and drop location bar in to add-on bar and Search bar(beside the Location bar) to the menu strip and click Done on the Customize Toolbar Dialog
4.Now bring up the Customize Toolbar Dialog (View->Toolbar->Customize...) again and click "Restore Default Set"
Actual Results:
The search bar come to it original position but the location bar is not restored to it original position from the add-on bar. Instead it go into "Customize toolbar"

Expected Results:
Everything must be restored back to normal position after clicking "restore default set"

All the item that are placed in the add-on bar are not restored. All of them go into the "Customize Toolbar" after clicking the "Restore Default Set"
