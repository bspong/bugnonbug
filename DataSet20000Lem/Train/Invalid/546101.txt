CMD+Q quits firefox, but doesn't store any of the browsing history of that session

CMD+Q quits firefox, but doesn't store any of the browsing history of that session. On reopening firefox, only the current history get stored and whatever wa being browsed before clicking CMD +Q doesnt get stored.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to couple of website in different tabs.
2. Click CMD+Q
3. Reopen firefox 3.6
Actual Results:
None of the browsing history is stored and displayed in the History dropdown.

Expected Results:
The history for the previous session before clicking cmd+q should be stored.
