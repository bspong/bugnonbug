Closing last tab doe not close window.
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060824 Minefield/3.0a1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060824 Minefield/3.0a1

When the preference "Always show the tab bar" is enabled. Closing the last tab doesn't close the window, it just clear the content of the tab.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Enable "Always show the tab bar"
2. Close the last tab.


Actual Results:
The content of the tab wa cleared.

Expected Results:
The window holding this tab should be closed.
