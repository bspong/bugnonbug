The wrap option is not memorized in the dialog of "Find in This Page..."
Although the wrap option of "Find in This Page" is memorized in Mozilla and
Camino, it doe not memorize in Firefox.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. select "Edit" --> "Find in This Page..."
2. The wrap option is turned OFF.
3. Execute "Find"
4. Close dialog
5. select "Edit" --> "Find in This Page..."

Actual Results:
The wrap option is turned ON.

Expected Results:
The wrap option is turned OFF.
