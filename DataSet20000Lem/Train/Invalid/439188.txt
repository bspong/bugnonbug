image thumbnail (thumb) generated when dragging an image can be a few pixel away from mouse pointer

Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.4; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008061004 Firefox/3.0

If you put your pointer at the very corner of an image and start dragging it the thumbnail can be floating a few pixel away.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Put your mouse on the very corner pixel of a large image.
2. Drag the image.
Actual Results:
The thumbnail wa floating a few pixel away from the pointer.

Expected Results:
The thumbnail should be positioned relative to the pointer AFTER being resized.

This is obviously very obscure but still technically a bug.
