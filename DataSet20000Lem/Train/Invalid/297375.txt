no connectivity of 1.0.4 with Anonymizer Total Net Security (SSH tunnelling proxy)
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.5) Gecko/20050519 Netscape/8.0.1

Upon installing the 1.0.4 security update, no connectivity wa available via
Anonymizer Total Net Security: for Firefox, Netscape 7.2, IE 6, RSS
browser-independent software, . . . (i.e., all). With all connectivity setting
the same, reverting to 1.0.3 solved the problem(s).

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Install 1.0.4 with Anonymizer Total Net Security already installed,
configured (connecting to internet) a well a Firefox 1.0.3 already installed.
2. Uninstall 1.0.3 / revert to 1.0.3.

Actual Results:
Problem vanished.

Expected Results:
1.0.4 should have connected a usual, but with the added security fixes.
