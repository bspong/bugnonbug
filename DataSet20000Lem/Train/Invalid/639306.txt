Tooltips misaligned (they are pushed to the left)
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; rv:2.0b12) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b12
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; rv:2.0b12) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b12

Tooltips over each image on the panel of image (lower center portion of the page www.maine.info), are all misaligned by being pushed substantially to the left of their respective images. These tooltips worked prior to FF Beta (i.e., FF 3.x) and work fine with every other browser.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to www.maine.info
2. Hover mouse over any image at lower center portion of page
3. See tooltip pop up to the LEFT of it image.
Actual Results:
Tooltip pop up substantially to the LEFT of it image.

Expected Results:
Tooltip should pop up CENTERED under it image.
