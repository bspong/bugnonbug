Summary: "Showing [name of bookmark folder]" doesn't update in real-time

Steps to Reproduce:

1. Open the Bookmarks Manager
2. Do a "Properties" on top-level bookmark folder
3. Change it name/title
4. Click "Save Changes"

At this point, you WILL see, correctly, change to the tree widget, dynamically reflected.

5. Examine the "Showing [name of bookmark folder]" heading

Expected Results:

"Showing [name of bookmark folder]" should also be updated dynamically

Actual Results:

To see your change reflected, you must close and re-open the Bookmarks Manager
