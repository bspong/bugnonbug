If I go to a website and add it a my bookmark by clicking on the yellow star in the URL bar and then add a tag, the tag doesn't work.

Example 1:
I go to http://www.mozilla.org
Then I hit the star icon and add a tag like "Mz"
Then I close the site and open a new tab and write "Mz" and hit Enter
Now it doe NOT open Mozilla.org, but instead it doe a Google search for "Mz"

If I do the same thing except adding the tag via the star icon, it works.

Example 2:
I go to http://www.mozilla.org/
I drag the site into a bookmark on my bookmark bar
Then I right click and select "Properties"
Then I add the tag "Mz"
Then I close the site and open a new tab and write "Mz" and hit Enter
Now it directs me to Mozilla.org

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. I go to http://www.mozilla.org
2. Then I hit the star icon and add a tag like "Mz"
3. Then I close the site and open a new tab and write "Mz" and hit Enter
4. Now it doe NOT open Mozilla.org, but instead it doe a Google search for "Mz"
Actual Results:
Doing a Google search for the tag

Expected Results:
Direct me to the bookmark using the tag
