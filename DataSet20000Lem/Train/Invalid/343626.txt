Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. open some tab so that the scrolling button appear
2. double-click on left or right button (enabled or disabled doesn't matter)
Actual Results:

Expected Results:
if button is enabled, scroll two tabs. if button is disabled, do nothing.
