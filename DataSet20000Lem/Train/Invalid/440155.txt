Tabs are only switched on the second click after closing another tab (LastTab v2.1 extension)
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0

When you have more than 3 tab opened and close one of them using the middle mouse button and then try to switch to another tab by clicking on it on the tab bar, it won't work on the first click - you need to click twice.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Have 3 or more tab opened
2. Close one of them using the middle mouse button
3. Click once on the inactive tab you want to switch to
Actual Results:
Nothing will happen. You need to click again to switch to the tab.

Expected Results:
Tab switching should happen on the first click.
