Technical expression on location bar
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0

http://img28.imageshack.us/img28/1860/switchtab.jpg

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. open a tab with the first website entry of location bar dropdown menu, e.g. "http://mozilla.org"
2. open the dropdown menu again and take a look at the first entry

Actual Results:
it not only like "http://mozilla.org" -> it "moz-action:switchtab,http://mozilla.org"

Expected Results:
just "http://mozilla.org"
