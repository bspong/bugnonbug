F6 failed to switch focus among different pane in firefox help window.
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050607 Firefox/1.0.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1

The focus can not be switched from different pane with pressing F6.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Launch Firefox.
2. Press F1 to open firefox help window.
3. Press F6 to switch focus among different panes, they are left pane, right pane and search text field.

Actual Results:
F6 failed to switch focus among different panes.

Expected Results:
F6 could switch focus among different panes.

Pressing Tab could change the focus position among different panes, F6 should work correctly too.
This bug can be seen on vermillion_07/snv_35 with Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1.
