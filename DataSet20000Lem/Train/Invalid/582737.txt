The url showed in a addressbar is not updated after a click on a bookmark link

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Surf to a webpage (eg www.google.com) --> Webpage is shown and the addressbar show google.com
2. Click on a bookmark link pointing to reader.google.com (url doesn't matter)
-->
a) reader Webpage is shown
b) Certificate next to the url show the new Certficate (e.g. Google reader)
c) url in the addressbar still show the old address (e.g. www.google.com)
d) title in the tab is correct and show the new webpage title
Actual Results:
Addressbar is not updated and show the previous address

Expected Results:
Addressbar is updated and show the current url
