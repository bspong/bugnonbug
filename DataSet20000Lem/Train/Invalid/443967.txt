Properties of a class/ID declared earlier in a parent class/ID are ignored.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Create element #parent whose p child are text-align: left.
2. Create sibling paragraph with class .child with text-align: center.
Actual Results:
All children, including .child, will have text-align: left.

Expected Results:
All child except .child should have text-align: left, who should have text-align: center.
