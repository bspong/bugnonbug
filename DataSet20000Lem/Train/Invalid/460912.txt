This bug ha also been reported to the developer of AIOS -- firefox.exxile.net.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Run Firefox 3.1 beta 2 pre
2.Right click on toolbar
3.Select "Customize..."
Actual Results:
Customize Toolbar open with no visible button to place on toolbars.
