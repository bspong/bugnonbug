During loading page with FireFox strange phenomenon wa observed.
Whole FireFox window is resizing in this way that it's width become bigger then
width of screen. [It's may be observed also on this page, and on gmail]
On 1024x768 when FireFox window is maximized content of whole window during
loading look like on 1100x768 or something like this.

It wa observed on 3 computer with resolution 1024x768 and 1280x1024.

On all those computer are instaled extensions:
googlebar 0.9.0.30
Adblock v.5 d2 * nightly 39
ForecastFox 0.7


Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Input any address in address bar [ex. www.google.com]
2. Hit enter
3. Observe FireFox window :-)

Actual Results:
During loading of page content of window wa resized to bigger width, after
loading it back to width 1024 px.

Expected Results:
Window shouldn't be resized during loading of pages.
