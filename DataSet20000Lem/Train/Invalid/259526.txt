Feed Location of Livemark not shown in Bookmarks Manager
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; rv:1.7.3) Gecko/20040914 Firefox/0.10
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; rv:1.7.3) Gecko/20040914 Firefox/0.10

The "Location" column in Bookmarks Manager, in case of a Livemark doe not show
the Feed Location. After changing a Livemak's property to another name and to
another feed, the entry in "Location" column remains unchanged and becomes junk.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Subscribe to an RSS Feed
2. Open Bookmark Manager and right-klick the new Livemark
3. From the context menu, select "Properties"
4. Change "Name" and overwrite "Feed Location" with
"http://www.theregister.co.uk/headlines.rss"
5. Confirm pressing "OK"
6. Open "Properties" againg and compare "Location" column with "Feed Location"
Actual Results:
"Location" column of Bookmaks Manager still showing old url of previous feed.

Expected Results:
"Location" column should list the new "Feed Location".
