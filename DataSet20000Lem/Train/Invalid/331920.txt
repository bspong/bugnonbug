Different setting in preference setting window need accelerate keys.
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050607 Firefox/1.0.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1

These setting are General, Privacy, Content, Tabs, Downloads and Advanced. They all have no accelerate keys.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Launch Firefox.
2. Go to top menu Edit->Preference to open the firefox preference setting window.

Actual Results:
General, Privacy, Content, Tabs, Downloads and Advanced setting tab have no accelerate keys.

Expected Results:
There should be an accelerate key for each different setting.

This bug could be found on vermilion_07/snv_35 with firefox version "Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1".
