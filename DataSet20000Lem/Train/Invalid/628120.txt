Flash video that are loaded into background tab now play automatically without focusing the tab
Flash video that are loaded into background tab are now played automatically even if the tab hasn't been focused. This changed within the last two weeks. The video used to not play at all until the tab wa made active.

STR
1) Go to Tools->Options->Tabs
2) Toggle pref "When I open a link in a new tab, switch to it immediately" to false
3) Ctrl+click this link http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yoajdUEYBIE&feature=feedu
4) Video start playing in the background tab
