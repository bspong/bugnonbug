Tabs change position when the Menu bar is missing
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US) AppleWebKit/534.13 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/9.0.597.98 Safari/534.13
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b12pre) Gecko/20110213 Firefox/4.0b12pre

If the menu bar is missing then when the window is minimized and maximized, the tab bar change position.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Select from View->Toolbars
2 Check : Navigation Toolbar; Bookmarks Toolbar; Tabs on Top
2. Maximize the window
3. Minimize the window
Actual Results:
On Maximize the Tabs are on the Title bar
On Minimize the Tabs are under the Title bar

Expected Results:
The tab should always be on the Title bar
