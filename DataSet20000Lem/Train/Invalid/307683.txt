Click on location or search bar doe not select URL/search term
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b4) Gecko/20050908 Firefox/1.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b4) Gecko/20050908 Firefox/1.4

In Firefox 1.0, clicking on the location bar selects the entire URL, and
clicking in the search bar selects the entire search term. In this new version,
clicking in either of these bar place the cursor at the mouse location,
instead of selecting the content of the bar.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Enter a URL into the location bar, or a search term into the search bar, and
hit enter
2. Click on the location/search bar
3.

Actual Results:
Cursor placed at mouse location

Expected Results:
Selected the entire content of the location/search bar

The keyboard shortcut Apple-L correctly selects the entire location bar URL, and
Apple-K selects the entire search term.
