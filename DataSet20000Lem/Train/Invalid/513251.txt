Upon installing and using Minefield (3.7a1pre), clicking bookmark fails to open the bookmarked page. This is true for the bookmark in the Bookmark Toolbar a well a standard bookmarks. Also true for right click, for both 'open in new window' and 'open in new tab'.

Apologies if this is already in the bug list; I did not see it while searching

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click a bookmark - no page open
2. Happens from any bookmark in the bookmark list or in the bookmark toolbar
3. Happens when left clicking or right clicking for new tab or new window
Actual Results:
no action whatsoever. bookmarked page do not open

Expected Results:
One expects a page to open upon clicking a bookmark.
