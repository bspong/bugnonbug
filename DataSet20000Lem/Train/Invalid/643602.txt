Opening A New Tab Problem While Window is Full Screen
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.0; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.0; rv:2.0) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0

When you use Firefox window in full screen, and try to open a new tab by clicking to near side of tab, it don't open a new tab and firefox window is resized.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Open a tab. (Tab 1)
2.Click to Maximize to Full Screen Window. (At the right corner of window)
3.Try to open a new tab by pressing right side of tab. (Tab 1)
4.It can't be opened.
5.Only thing that is resizing the window like "Restore Down" button effect.

6.When it is resized window, it start to work! When you click right side of tab, a new tab will be opened.
7.As you see, you can't open a new tab when window is full sized.


Actual Results:
We can't open new tab by clicking near side of tab when window is fully sized.

Expected Results:
We should open new tab by clicking near side of tab.
