Screenshot

Toggling from 'Tabs on Top' to tab on bottom and then back to 'Tabs on Top' causing a reduction in the height of the titlebar which also cut of the top of tab and the Firefox button.

STR:
1) Have the option in View->Toolbars->Tabs on Top enabled
2) Toggle the option to put tab on the bottom
3) Toggle the option to put tab back on the top

Note the top of tab and the Firefox button are cut off and the distance between the bottom of the Firefox button and the navigation bar is reduced by five pixels.
