dropped tab from other firefox window won't close [caused by Google Toolbar]
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.0; it; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6 (.NET CLR 3.5.30729)
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.0; it; rv:1.9.2) Gecko/20100115 Firefox/3.6 GTB6 (.NET CLR 3.5.30729)

When a tab that is dropped from another window is closed, it disappears from the tab on firefox, but:

CTRL+TAB still can access it
when closing the window, firefox tell that there are more tab still open
you can't close it in any way, you can only close the entire window

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Open 2 firefox window
2.Drag the tab from the first window to the second
3.Now you have 1 window with 2 tab
4.Close the dropped tab
5.Press CTRL+TAB, or the right button on the tab panel, to access the "closed" tab
Actual Results:
Got a window with 1 visible tab and 1 hidden tab

Expected Results:
Close the second tab

Tried on 2 PC with different configuration, same result
