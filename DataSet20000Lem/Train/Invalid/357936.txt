'open all in tabs' on folder loses existing tab
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0

When opening a folder of bookmark from the toolbar menu (right click - open all in tab or middle click folder), it us existing tab and close any others.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Right click on a folder in the bookmark toolbar menu & select "Open all in tabs" or Middle click on a folder in the bookmark toolbar menu

Actual Results:
Existing tab are used and any others are closed.

Expected Results:
Should use new tab instead.
