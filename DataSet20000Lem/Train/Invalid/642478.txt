Prerequisites: 2 Facebook user that are friend on Facebook (i.e. UserA and UserB)

4. UserB - Select "Hide this post" ==> Expected\Actual result#1
5. UserB - After the post is hidden - Click on News Feed
6. UserB - Go to Top News and Most Recent page ==> Expected\Actual result#2
Actual Results:
** Actual result#1
Only the selected post wa hidden in UserB News feed page

** Actual result#2:
All Firefox badge post were hidden from UserB Top News and Most Recent page



Expected Results:
** Expected result#1:
Only the selected post will be hidden in UserB News Feed page

** Expected result#1:
Only the selected post will be hidden in UserB Top News and News Feed page
