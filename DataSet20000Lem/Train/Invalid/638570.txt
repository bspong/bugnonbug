The past selected split menu is highlighted when open the Firefox button again
Build Identifier:
Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0b13pre) Gecko/20110303 Firefox/4.0b13pre ID:20110303030406

The past selected split menu is highlighted when open the Firefox button again.
This happens on Aero and Classic.


Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Start Minefield with new profile
2. Click Firefox Button
3. Click Options ">" and Menu Bar
4. Click View > Toolbars > Menu Bar
5. Click Firefox Button

Actual Results:
The past selected split menu is highlighted

Expected Results:
The past selected split menu should not be highlighted
