Read topic randomly become marked unread when clicking any of it reply and then doing a forum refresh.

Here's a way to reproduce it:

http://www.pcx-forum.de/pxmboard.php?mode=board&brdid=1

Click on any topic and any of it reply in the middle frame. Then do a refresh of that forum area. The topic of the reply you've read will suddenly be marked a unread. If you only click on any topic without reading any of it replies, these topic will still be marked a read.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Browse any forum that's using PXMBoard software.
2. Click on any topic and any of it reply in the middle frame.
3. Do a refresh of that forum area. The topic of the reply you've read will suddenly be marked a unread.
Actual Results:
Read topic will be displayed a unread.

Expected Results:
The topic should be marked a read.
