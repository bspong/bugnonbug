Enable or disable any extension and choose RESTART button, the above site loop continuously trying to display content. The issue can be repeated with 100% success rate. If I restart Firefox manually, the above site open a expected. Error in the RESTART button in Add-Ons option.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Make the following web site the default start page: https://mypima.pima.edu/cp/home/displaylogin
2. Add an add-on (Better YouTube is a good test) and click on the RESTART button in the add-on/extensions/themes window. Approve the restarting of Firefox.
3. On restart, Firefox loop continuously showing "connecting" and flashing progress bar until the STOP button is pressed.
Actual Results:
Firefox loop continuously showing "connecting" and flashing progress bar until the STOP button is pressed.

Expected Results:
The above URL should have opened without hesitation, and not continuously looped with "connecting" and progress bar flashing.

The above action can be resolved by closing the Add-ons window and simply quitting Firefox and starting again.
