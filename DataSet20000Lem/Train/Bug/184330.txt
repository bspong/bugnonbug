Unable to right click toolbar bookmark when bookmark are under the Chevron
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.3a) Gecko/20021207 Phoenix/0.5
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.3a) Gecko/20021207 Phoenix/0.5

When bookmark on the toolbar are to long for the toolbar itself and extent
under the ">>" arrows, the right click option do not appear. This make it
clunky when trying to edit these bookmarks. The bookmark also do not move into
the toolbar when there is room.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Create multiple bookmark on the toolbar that invoke the ">>" button for more
toolbar favorites.
2.Edit toolbar favorite that appear in the toolbar to have a shorter name (eg,
userfriendly to UF)
3.Bookmarks do not shift in when there is room.
4.Click the ">>" button to access other bookmark that do not appear
5.Right click on bookmarks, the right click option doe not appear

Actual Results:
Bookmarks do not shift in and right click option do not appear

Expected Results:
The next bookmark will shift in when there is room.
Right click option appears when right clicking bookmark that are under ">>" button

default theme and other theme same result. Bookmarks shift in when changing themes.
