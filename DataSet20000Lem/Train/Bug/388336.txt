New live bookmark feed in bookmark toolbar doesn't load until manually reloaded.
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9a7pre) Gecko/2007071605 Minefield/3.0a7pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9a7pre) Gecko/2007071605 Minefield/3.0a7pre

When a feed is subscribed to using the orange feed icon to the right hand side of the address bar it content do not load until "Reload Live Bookmark" is selected from the context menu brought up by a right hand mouse button click.

Until "Reload live bookmark" is selected the feed show a greyed out "(Empty)"

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Visit a page with a linked RSS feed
2. Click orange feed icon on right hand side of address bar.
3. Keep default suggested name and subscribe in bookmark toolbar folder
4. Click on live bookmark in the bookmark toolbar
Actual Results:
feed show a "(Empty)" until manually reloaded

Expected Results:
on subscribe a list of feed should have been retrieved and displayed, and "Live Bookmark loading" displayed until the list of feed ha been retrieved.

WORKAROUND:

right click and select "Reload Live Bookmark", this will load list of feeds.
