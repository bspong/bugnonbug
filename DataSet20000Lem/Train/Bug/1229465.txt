browser/fuel/test/browser_Browser.js leak window when a marionette session is running
To satisfy addons signing, the mochitest harness will need to use marionette for bootstrapping (instead of relying on xul overlays). But browser/fuel/test/browser_Browser.js leak window when marionette is enabled.

STR:
1. Apply attached patch
2. ./mach mochitest browser/fuel/test/browser_Browser.js
