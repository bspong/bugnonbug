The Truck and Bus Control and Communications Network Committee is responsible for developing, reviewing and approving standard and information report related to communication network for the exchange of control and diagnostic information among electronic component on or connected to truck and bus vehicles. The objective is the architecture of communication network offering an open interconnect system that support communication between loose package components, such a engine and transmissions, which are sourced from many different component supplier and integrated into vehicle systems. The communication network are developed for use in on-highway and off-highway truck and their trailers, buses, construction equipment, and agricultural equipment and implements, and applicable to light-duty, medium-duty, and heavy-duty vehicle used on-road or off-road, and for appropriate stationary application which use vehicle derived component (e.g. generator sets).
