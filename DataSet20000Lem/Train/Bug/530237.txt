Cannot scroll the Excel Web App by clicking the empty space in the scrollbars
User-Agent: Mozilla/4.0 (compatible; MSIE 8.0; Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; Trident/4.0; SLCC2; .NET CLR 2.0.50727; .NET CLR 3.5.30729; .NET CLR 3.0.30729; Media Center PC 6.0; MS-RTC LM 8; InfoPath.3)
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.1; en-US; rv:1.9.2b3) Gecko/20091115 Firefox/3.6b3

When using Microsoft's Excel Web App with FireFox 3.6 Beta 3, you cannot scroll the app by clicking in the empty space in the scrollbars. This work in all other browsers, including FireFox 3.5.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to http://skydrive.live.com. Log in with your credentials.
2. Upload an Excel workbook (xlsx or xlsb format) if you don’t have one already up there.
2a. If this is your first time, you will see a "Join our preview program" bar appear. Click that link.
2b. Click Accept
3. Click on the workbook
4. Click View
5. After your workbook loads, click in the blank portion of the scrollbar or on the actual scrollbar arrow
Actual Results:
The workbook doe not scroll. Note that it doe work if you click and drag the scrollbar thumb.

Expected Results:
For the workbook to scroll like it doe in all other browsers, including FF3.5.
