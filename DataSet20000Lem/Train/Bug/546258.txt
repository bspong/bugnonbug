Too much time is taken for opening very long pages. Even they completely downloaded.

(When many page are opened), plus you try to open Very Long Pages then switch between them it take much time for see next tab. But it possible to do it by minimizing application and restore again.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open some page
2. Open also a very long page simultaneously
For example:
http://www.mobile-review.com/review/samsung-u900soul.shtml#9
http://www.mobile-review.com/review/samsung-d900.shtml
http://www.mobile-review.com/review/lg-gd330.shtml
3. Try to switch between tab
(3.1) It wait too long, but it's possible to switch on it by minimizing the application and restore again
4. wait until they be fully downloaded
5. Try to switch between tab
(5.1) It wait too long also, but it's still possible to switch on it by minimizing the application and restore again
Actual Results:
Too much time is taken for switching between tabs. About 5-15 second for not opened page and 3-5 sec for downloaded.

Expected Results:
Immediate opening of the tabs. (Without minimizing the application).
