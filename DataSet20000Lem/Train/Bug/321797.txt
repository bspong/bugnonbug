Moving bookmark at bottom of list (via Bookmarks menu) to another portion of the list, cause the bookmark list not to scroll further and to eventually disappear.

Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8) Gecko/20051111 Firefox/1.5

When it reach the up-arrow, the bookmark list doe not scroll up beyond that point, a circle with diagonal line appears, the entire list disappears, and the "Bookmarks" menu title remains higlighted.

1.Save new bookmark, it default to the bottom of the list.
2.Left-click the new bookmark to drag it further up in the list.
3.When it reach the up-arrow, the list doe not scroll beyond that point and will disappear, leaving the "Bookmarks" menu title highlighted.

Actual Results:
Bookmark list did not scroll up, menu title remained higlighted even after the mouse wa moved off.

Expected Results:
Bookmarks list should have scrolled up allowing for the correct placement of the new bookmark.
