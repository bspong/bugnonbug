The test URL show a 6-frame looping Doppler radar display. A new frame is added every 3 - 5 minute or so, and the page refreshes every 6 minutes. Going to another page and then back to the original one sometimes load a stale version of the image.

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Let the test URL sit for 10 or 15 minute so the image is reloaded with a new frame at least once. For example, if it showed time 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 in the upper-left corner at first, we want it to show time 2 - 7 or 3 - 8 now.
2. Click any link on the page.
3. Go back to the previous page.
Actual Results:
A stale, older image is loaded from cache, e.g. showing time 1 - 6 instead of the more recent 2 - 7 or 3 - 8.

Expected Results:
The most recent version of the animation should be shown.
