Continuous Places activity while idle reduces laptop battery life
User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux x86_64; en-US; rv:1.9.0.1) Gecko/2008071718 Firefox/3.0.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux x86_64; en-US; rv:1.9.0.1) Gecko/2008071718 Firefox/3.0.1

Firefox 3.0.1 doe continuously modify places.sqlite and places.sqlite-journal while tab are open and the browser is otherwise idle (tcpdump show no firefox related activity).
This happens at irregular interval of a few minutes, usually at least once within five minutes.
This prevents the use of disk spin down on laptop and thus cause reduced battery life.

This mean that the frecency entry of moz_places is modified. This modification should only be done when the browser becomes active e.g. when a wb page is reloaded.


Reproducible: Always




Don't update on disk data when there is apparently no need to do so to preserve laptop battery life and to allow disk spin down.
