Opening a new tab does not display the new webpage by default
User-Agent:       
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.5) Gecko/20031007 Firebird/0.7

Everytime you right click on a link in an existing tabbed window and click "Open
link in new tab", the new link is loaded in the new tab but the browser remains
on the existing tabbed window. 

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Find a link anywhere in the existing document
2. Right click on the link and select "Opne link in new tab"
3. New tab loads in the background but does not appear on screen by default
until you click on the new tab.
