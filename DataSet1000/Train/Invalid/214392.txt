Bookmarks will not sort  after sorting them in Manage Bookmarks
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.5a) Gecko/20030728 Mozilla Firebird/0.6.1
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.5a) Gecko/20030728 Mozilla Firebird/0.6.1

After sorting bookmarks in the Manage Bookmarks window by name, the list is not
sorting when using Bookmarks in the Menu Bar.  Also, new folders are not sorted
as well and appear at the top of the list.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.  Add new folder to bookmarks, with a site included.
2.  Sort list by name and close Manage Bookmarks.
3.  Use Bookmark menu and notice bookmarks are not sorted by name.  New folder
is at the top of the list.

Actual Results:  
Results are identified in Reproduce step #3.

Expected Results:  
Displayed sorted bookmarks.  Noticed this problem when Netscape went to 6.0.

MozillaFirebird theme.  Adblock and Google extension.
