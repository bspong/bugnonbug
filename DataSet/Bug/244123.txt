Menu Buttons cease functioning after an indeterminate period. Unable to work around "save" "cancel" etc in any menu.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.6) Gecko/20040113
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.6) Gecko/20040113

Menu buttons cease to function after variable period of time after application
is launched.  The functionality reappears after a force quit and relaunch.  This
bug prevents saving a book mark, compacting folders, closing tabs--any action
where a menu is closed by a button.  Radio buttons do opperate a selection.  The
button will visually appear to function(e.g color change and blink on a mouse
click) however there is no response to the command. This is true for mail,
perferences and browser opperations. In my case the Bug appeared after going to
1.6. This bug will happen 90% of the time if the application is running for over
3 hours.  Partial Crash Logs are as follows: These may or may not be the
scessions involved.
