When the mouse is hovered on top of say the tabs or the tab close button or back/forward keys etc, the resulting tool tip occupies the screen starting from the mouse pointer down till the end of the screen (even outside the firefox window).

1. Open a new Firefox instance
2. open a new tab
3. visit www.google.com
4. hover mouse on top of the google tab
Actual Results:

Expected Results:  
tooltip whose size is just bigger than the text that is being displayed
