Two radio buttons can be selected simultaneously for the same item

At http://www.davisairporter.com/, two opposing radio buttons can be selected simultaneously.  This should not be possible.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.  Go to http://www.davisairporter.com/
2.  Click on To SMF
3.  Click "no" on the round trip radio button.  Then click "yes".  Both are selected.
Actual Results:  
Both no and yes are selected.

Expected Results:  
Only one should be selected.
