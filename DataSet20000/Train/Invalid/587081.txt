TabView first run video group disappears after first run
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.0; rv:2.0b4pre) Gecko/20100813 Minefield/4.0b4pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.0; rv:2.0b4pre) Gecko/20100813 Minefield/4.0b4pre

On first showing TabView the group with an introductory video is shown. If the video is ignored or watched and the tab group left then it will have disappeared the next time you load firefox and go into TabView

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. open firefox with clean profile, click on TabView
2. watch or ignore intro video, then close firefox
3. open firefox and TabView
Actual Results:  
Intro video group has gone with no way to get it back.

Expected Results:  
Video group should be available until explicitly deleted by the user.
