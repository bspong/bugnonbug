When I switch from Firefox to a different window, and try to manipulate 2nd window with the title bar, Firefox steals the focus. This happens any time I click the title bar on the 2nd window. 

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Firefox
2. Open second GUI app
3. Click title bar in 2nd app
Actual Results:  
Firefox moves to front

Expected Results:  
Able to move/resize/close 2nd window

Similar behavior has been reported by Windows Vista users on Mozilla support site at

https://support.mozilla.com/en-US/questions/697324

It's happening to me using openSUSE Linux with Gnome DE.
