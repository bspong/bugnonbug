2.Go to ftp://ftp.mozilla.org/pub/mozilla.org/firefox/nightly/3.0b3-candidates/rc2/
3. Download en-US.win32.installer.exe update
4.Install as normal

Home button not present on  Fx3B3

Select 'Restore default set...' then switch back to Fx3B3RC2.
Home button then visible on Fx3B3, however when clicking on 'Restore default set...' removes the Home button.

Navigation toolbar should show home button.
