ER: Middle-click submenu items under "more" dropdown on Google.com does not open a new tab
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1.14) Gecko/20080404 Firefox/2.0.0.14
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1.14) Gecko/20080404 Firefox/2.0.0.14

If the user tries to middle-click any submenu item under the "more" menu on www.google.com menus, and if the middle-click is assigned to open a new tab, nothing occurs.  This would be an enhancement request.  May require some collaboration with Google folks.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to http://www.google.com.
2. Find the "more" link, left-click, "more" dropdown, highlight any "more" menu item, middle-click mouse (middle-click assigned to create a new tab)
3. Observe, the middle click has no effect on any submenu item. 
Actual Results:  
Actual:  Middle-click has no effect.

Expected Results:  
Expected:  Middle-click to open a new tab when selecting a submenu item from the "more" dropdown from www.google.com.
