Version in menu Help>About is misleading
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0

In Firefox 3 RC1 and RC2, the version windows shows "Firefox 3.0"

It needs to show "Firefox 3.0 RC2", "Firefox 3.0 betaxx", "Firefox 3.0.0.0.-2", or other more human readable string, because otherwise people will run beta or Release Candidate versions, without knowing they need to update.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click on Help menu
2. Then click on "About Mozilla Firefox"
3.Read Version under Firefox title
Actual Results:  
Show "Firefox 3.0".

Expected Results:  
something like "Firefox 3.0 RC2".

is release candidate 2
