Flash videos that are loaded into background tabs now play automatically without focusing the tab
Flash videos that are loaded into background tabs are now played automatically even if the tab hasn't been focused.  This changed within the last two weeks.  The videos used to not play at all until the tab was made active.

STR
1) Go to Tools->Options->Tabs
2) Toggle pref "When I open a link in a new tab, switch to it immediately" to false
3) Ctrl+click this link http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yoajdUEYBIE&feature=feedu
4) Video starts playing in the background tab
