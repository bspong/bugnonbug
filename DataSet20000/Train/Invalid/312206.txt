Aligning Center for an Iframe does not work
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.12) Gecko/20050915 Firefox/1.0.7
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.12) Gecko/20050915 Firefox/1.0.7

Setting the align attribute of an iframe to center or middle does not align the
iframe to the center.  Setting it to left or right does work however.  

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
<html>
<body>
Hello<BR>
<iframe align=right width=200 height=200 src='http://edition.cnn.com'>
No Support of IFRAME
</iframe>
</body>
</html>
Actual Results:  
Iframe is aligned to the left instead of center

Expected Results:  
Iframe should be aligned to the center
