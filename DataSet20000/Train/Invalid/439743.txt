Video chat does not work with Firefox 3 in Meebo
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.0; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 6.0; en-US; rv:1.9) Gecko/2008052906 Firefox/3.0

The video chat feature in Meebo can not be "accepted" from Firefox 3.  The buttons do not "click." 

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.  Log into Meebo
2.  Attempt to initiate a video chat
3.  Attempt to "accept" the access to your webcam.
Actual Results:  
The accept button could not be "pushed."

Expected Results:  
Access the webcam to allow the person with whom I am chatting to see and hear me.
