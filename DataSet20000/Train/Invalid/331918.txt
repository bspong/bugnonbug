Accelerate keys are missing in preference setting tabs.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux i686; en-US; rv:1.7.8) Gecko/20050607 Firefox/1.0.4
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1

In preference setting, there are several tabs under privacy and advanced setting. But the accelerate key for each tab is missing.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Launch firefox and go to preference setting window.
2. Select Privacy setting and Advanced setting.

Actual Results:  
In Privacy and Advanced setting, there are several tabs included. For each tab, accelerate key is missing.

Expected Results:  
Every tab should have its unique accelerate key.

This bug can be reproduced on vermillion_07/snv_35 with Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; SunOS sun4u; en-US; rv:1.9a1) Gecko/20060326 Firefox/1.6a1.
