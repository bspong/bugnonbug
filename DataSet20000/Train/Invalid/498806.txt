FF window moves when resizing Google Toolbar Google search box.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.5; en-US; rv:1.9.1) Gecko/20090615 Firefox/3.5 GTB5
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; Intel Mac OS X 10.5; en-US; rv:1.9.1) Gecko/20090615 Firefox/3.5 GTB5

When you resize the Google search field in the toolbar by dragging the divider between the search field and the other toolbar buttons. The search field does resize, but the whole FF window moves as well.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Install Google Toolbar
2. Drag the divider between the Google search field and the buttons to resize the field
Actual Results:  
The google search field resizes, but the FF window moves in the direction you drag the divider as well.

Expected Results:  
The FF window should not move.
