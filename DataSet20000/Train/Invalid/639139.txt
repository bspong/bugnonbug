When closing Firefox window momentarily flashes after disappearing
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b12) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b12
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 5.1; rv:2.0b12) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b12

Whenever I close Firefox, the window and everything disappears but I see a momentary flash of the screen immediately. This is happening only in Firefox 4.0 Beta 12 but not in Beta 11 and previous versions.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Close Firefox
2. Watch the window disappear
3. Immediately expect to see a momentary flash
Actual Results:  
A momentary flash appeared

Expected Results:  
Nothing must appear
