Just a heads up to anyone using the FF 3.6.8 update. Upon downloading this update it removed my Google navigation menus (ie: the upper left menu containing images, videos, maps, news, gmail etc...) If you mouse over you can find the links but the actual icons are missing. After much troubleshooting I uninstalled 3.6.8 and re-installed an earlier version (3.6.3) and the Google menus are now back.
