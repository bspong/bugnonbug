The new daily of Minefield alpha 4 shows a new bug not present in previous alphas. The address bar's content doesn't change URIs when opening a new page.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Minefield alpha 4
2. Open a website
3. Click on a link to move to another page
Actual Results:  
The address bar stayed at the old URI

Expected Results:  
The address bar should update to reflect the new URI

You can click in the address bar, then press Escape key, then the URI will update to the correct one.
