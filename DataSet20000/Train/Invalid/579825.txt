Transparent background on drop-menu when right click over adblock addon
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64; en-US; rv:2.0b2pre) Gecko/20100718 Ubuntu/10.04 (lucid) MozillaDeveloperPreview/4.0b2pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64; en-US; rv:2.0b2pre) Gecko/20100718 Ubuntu/10.04 (lucid) MozillaDeveloperPreview/4.0b2pre

If you right click over adblock plus v1.2.1 (on settings, icon, etc) the drop menu will be transparent. The drop menu created when using left click works without problem.
This bug happen on Firefox 4.0b2pre but works fine on Firefox 3.6.8pre.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Install adblock plus addon
2. Right click over the icon on the toolbar or on the settings (of the addon) or another list of filters.

Actual Results:  
Menu with transparent background.

Expected Results:  
Menu with normal background.

Using default theme.
