Status bar vanishes in full screen mode.
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X; en-us) AppleWebKit/125.5.5 (KHTML, like Gecko) Safari/125.11
Build Identifier: Firefox 0.9.1+

Status bar vanishes in full screen mode. Turning off full screen mode brings the status bar back. Note: I 
am using Mac OS X 10.3.6 on a dual G5 2.0 ghz, 2.5 mb RAM, build of Firefox as of 16-Nov-2004.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go to View and turn on Full Screen
2. Note loss of status bar (bottom of screen)
3. To recover status bar (assuming it was checked in the first place), turn off Full Screen
Actual Results:  
Status bar vanished.

Expected Results:  
Leave the status bar in place, or not, depending on the toolbar view setting for the object.
