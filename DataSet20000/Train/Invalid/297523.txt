The cursor in text field blinks very fast on Flash movie transation effects.
When the Flash movie is still, the cursor blinks normally. But when the Flash
movie is showing any transition effects (from one image to other etc.) the
cursor blinks very very fast.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Go the URL http://www.airtelworld.com
2. Wait till the page the completely loaded and click inside the USERNAME text
field.
3. Just watch the cursor blinking when the Flash movie is still, and when the
Flash movie is transitioning one image to another.
