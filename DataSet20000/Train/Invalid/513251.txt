Upon installing and using Minefield (3.7a1pre), clicking bookmarks fails to open the bookmarked page.  This is true for the bookmarks in the Bookmark Toolbar as well as standard bookmarks.  Also true for right click, for both 'open in new window' and 'open in new tab'.

Apologies if this is already in the bug list;  I did not see it while searching

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click a bookmark - no page opens
2. Happens from any bookmark in the bookmarks list or in the bookmarks toolbar
3. Happens when left clicking or right clicking for new tab or new window
Actual Results:  
no action whatsoever.  bookmarked pages do not open

Expected Results:  
One expects a page to open upon clicking a bookmark.
