Flash Ads Disappear When Scrolling or When Mouse-Over to Another Tab (See Video)
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0b11pre) Gecko/20110127 Firefox/4.0b11pre
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows NT 6.1; WOW64; rv:2.0b11pre) Gecko/20110127 Firefox/4.0b11pre

Flash ads will disappear and reappear on certain pages, such as (http://www.patsajakgames.com/games/psgcc_online.html).

Reproducible: Sometimes

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open a page with Flash Ads (such as http://www.patsajakgames.com/games/psgcc_online.html).
2. Scroll the page all the way down and back up.  Ad on right disappears.
3. Flash ad disappears and reappears.
4. Sometimes the ad disappears by just moving the mouse down from the current tab, inside the window area, then back up to another tab.
Actual Results:  
Flash ad disappears and reappears.

Expected Results:  
Flash ad should remain visible.
