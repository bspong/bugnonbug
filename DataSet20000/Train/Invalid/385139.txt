"Showing [name of bookmarks folder]" doesn't update in real-time
I spun this off of bug 384623.

Summary: "Showing [name of bookmarks folder]" doesn't update in real-time

Steps to Reproduce:

1. Open the Bookmarks Manager
2. Do a "Properties" on top-level bookmarks folder
3. Change its name/title
4. Click "Save Changes"

At this point, you WILL see, correctly, changes to the tree widget, dynamically reflected.

5. Examine the "Showing [name of bookmarks folder]" heading

Expected Results:

"Showing [name of bookmarks folder]" should also be updated dynamically

Actual Results:

To see your changes reflected, you must close and re-open the Bookmarks Manager
