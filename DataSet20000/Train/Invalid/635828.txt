Mouse over on a link causes the location text in the location bar to move up a couple of pixels
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; Intel Mac OS X 10.6; rv:2.0b11) Gecko/20100101 Firefox/4.0b11

Any link in Firefox 4 beta on Mac seems to cause this problem. Move the mouse over a link and the text in the location bar moves up a couple of pixels. Doesn't happen on Firefox on Windows

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Visit http://bugzilla.mozilla.org
2. Mouse over any link in the text
3. Look at the text in the location bar
Actual Results:  
Text moves up two pixels during mouse over and moves down after mouse out

Expected Results:  
Text remains in the same position.
