Close tab icon does not appear on hover over a tab if more than 7 tabs are open
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.8.1) Gecko/20061010 Firefox/2.0

If more than 7 tabs are open close tab icon does not appear on a tab when u hover on top of it. If 7 or less tabs are open the close tab icon appears.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open multiple tabs 7 or less, can be blank windows.
2. Hover on top of a tab. The tab should be highlighted and close tab icon should appear when you move the mouse to the right corner of the tab. 
3. Open more than 7 tabs. Repeat step 2. close tab icon does not appear.

Actual Results:  
Close tab icon does not appear on mouse over on the right of a tab when more than 7 tabs are open.

Expected Results:  
Close tab icon should have been activated on mouse over on the right of a tab when more than 7 tabs are open.
