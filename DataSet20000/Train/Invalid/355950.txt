When multiple tabs are open, each tab has two "x" (close tab) buttons shown that are slightly overlapped. Only one of the two "x" buttons works to close the tab.

Operating System: Mac OS X 10.4.8

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Launch FireFox
2. Open a new tab
3. Look at the "x" button for the tab

Actual Results:  
Two "x" buttons were shown per tab.

Expected Results:  
Only one "x" button should be shown per tab.
