Advanced/Browsing Preferences box forgets state
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.5) Gecko/20041103 Firefox/1.0RC2
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.1; en-US; rv:1.7.5) Gecko/20041103 Firefox/1.0RC2

The Browsing box under Advanced Preferences stays open regardless of what state
it was in when you left the tab.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open Options/Advanced.
2. Close the "Browsing" box.
3. Switch to another tab (e.g. Privacy), then back to Advanced.

Actual Results:  
"Browsing" options box is open again.

Expected Results:  
Box remains closed.
