Blue line around tabs when you click the tab a second time
User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b2) Gecko/20050604 Firefox/1.0+ (PowerBook)
Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Macintosh; U; PPC Mac OS X Mach-O; en-US; rv:1.8b2) Gecko/20050604 Firefox/1.0+ (PowerBook)

A blue line around the tab appear when you first select a tab, then click it a
second time.
It first appeared in the 2005-06-01-07 nightly build (working fine in the
2005-05-31-13 nightly build).


Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Click "File > New Tab" to display the Tab bar.
2. Click a tab to select it.
3. Click the same tab a second time.
Actual Results:  
A blue line appear around the tab

Expected Results:  
No change in the tab appearance
