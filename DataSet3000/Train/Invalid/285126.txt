Empty display resolution box when non-default value set
After clicking OK and reopening, the "Display resolution" dropdown in the
advanced fonts panel appears blank for all resolutions except 92 dpi.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Goto Tools->Options->Content->Advanced (in the Fonts & Colors section).
2. Change the "Display resolution" from 96 dpi to 72 dpi.
3. Click the OK button for the Fonts dialog.
4. Click Advanced again.

Expected Results
"Display resolution" should display 72dpi.

Actual Results
"Display resolution" is empty.
