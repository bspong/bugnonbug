"Force links that open new windows..." checkbox does not remeber state when changing panels in options

Build Identifier: Mozilla/5.0 (Windows; U; Windows NT 5.0; en-US; rv:1.7.3) Gecko/20041015 Firefox/0.10

.

Reproducible: Always
Steps to Reproduce:
1. Tools->Options.
2. Click Advanced.
3. tick or untick "Force links that open new windows to open in" checkbox.
4. Click back to any tab on the left, e.g. General, Privacy,Web Features and
Downloads.
5. Click OK.
6. Open Options again, and the checkbox does not remember the state that chosen
at last.
